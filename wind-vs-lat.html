Wind Speed vs Latitude



Observations


There does seem to be a correlation between wind speed and the equator. There is a larger grouping of lower wind speeds closer to the equator and if we were to draw a line through the dataset, it appears as though the wind speed stays low, but also does tend to get higher as the latitude moves positively away from the equator.
